About Woodinville Connect

Building stronger connections across our community.

Our Mission

Woodinville Connect was created to provide residents with a centralized, easy-to-use platform that highlights local resources, support services, and community events. Our goal is to make it simple for individuals and families to find the help and opportunities they need.

Why This Matters

Communities thrive when people know where to turn for assistance. From food security and healthcare to youth programs and volunteer opportunities, access to reliable information strengthens neighborhoods and empowers residents.

Community Impact

By organizing local services into one searchable hub, Woodinville Connect helps reduce barriers to access and increases awareness of valuable programs. We aim to support residents, promote civic engagement, and encourage collaboration among community organizations.

Created For

This website was developed as part of the TSA Webmaster Competition to serve the Woodinville community and demonstrate the power of accessible digital tools in strengthening local connections.
